Udi's Gluten Free

Udi's Gluten Free Community was one of the most active and loyal branded BlogFrog communities. When BlogFrog pivoted to TapInfluence, support for the community product was dropped. Because of the significant value and loyal users of the Udi's Gluten Free Community, the TCS team (four of us) rebuilt an external community product from scratch in two months, pro bono. The rebuilt product included an administrative GUI that hooked into the TapInfluence platform.

Technologies used: Expression Engine, Backbone.js, TapInfluence. Community was embedded via iframe into Udi's Gluten Free website.

Microsoft - Nokia Lumia 928

To promote the new Nokia Lumia 928, we created an interactive comparison widget that was embedded within blogs. Bloggers took three sets of two photos each; one photo with an iPhone 5, and the other with the Nokia Lumia 928. The comparison widget would display each set of photos side-by-side, and the reader would then choose the photo that s/he thought looked best. The final frame of the widget was dependent upon whether the photo from the Nokia Lumia 928 was chosen 2/3 times or not.

Technologies used: TapInfluence Platform, HTML, CSS, Javascript

  • Client: Microsoft / Windows Phone / Nokia Lumia 928
  • Date: May 2013
  • Responsibilities: Designs, userflow, HTML, CSS, some JavaScript
